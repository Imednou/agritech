AGRITECH

AGRITECH offers a complete range of devices to control your houses, livestock buildings and greenhouses in order to meet your specific comfort, security needs and animal and plant well-being. These devices are adaptable according to your projects. The AGRITECH modules are particularly user-friendly and configurable: they can adapt to your needs and they are designed for your profession. Their interfaces allow simple and quick navigation through menus and screens.
